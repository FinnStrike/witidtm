Lecture/Online Session Schedule

It's almost all online for the start of the semester, but hopefully we'll get face to face sessions in the not too distant.

You can join sessions via the blackboard collaborate ultra tool.

Our regular lecture slots are:

  • Tuesday 1000-1050 online
  • Thursday 1600-1750 online

We'll do an "office hours" session on Monday's at 1100, just drop in to the "Monday office hours" blackboard session, and chat as you want. We can swap the timeslot later in the semester depending on how it conflicts with people's schedules.
